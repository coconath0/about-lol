Riot Games is an American video game developer and publisher known for creating League of Legends, one of the most popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) games in the world. Launched in 2009, League of Legends has grown into a massive global phenomenon, attracting millions of players and a vibrant esports community. The game's continuous updates and expansions ensure that players remain engaged and challenged.

In League of Legends, players assume the role of "champions" and compete in teams to destroy the opposing team's Nexus, the heart of their base. Each champion has unique abilities and playstyles, promoting strategic gameplay and teamwork. The game features various modes, including ranked matches and casual play, catering to both competitive and casual gamers.

Beyond gameplay, Riot Games has established a rich lore and universe for League of Legends. The game's narrative is built around the champions, each with their own backstories, motivations, and relationships. Additionally, Riot Games has expanded the franchise through animated series, comics, and merchandise, further deepening the connection between players and the League of Legends universe.
